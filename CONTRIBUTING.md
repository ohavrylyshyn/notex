# Contributing to NoteX

Thank you for your interest in contributing to NoteX.

## Contribution Policy

Due to security and ethical reasons related to Russia's armed aggression
against Ukraine, we do not accept contributions from individuals or
organizations affiliated with the Russian Federation or those supporting
Russia's war against Ukraine.

All contributions are subject to manual review.
Maintainers reserve the right to reject any contribution without explanation.

By submitting a contribution, you confirm that you comply with this policy.
